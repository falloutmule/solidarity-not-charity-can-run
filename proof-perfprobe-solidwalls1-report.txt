solidwalls1 gated perfprobe overlay
===================================

Enable (public):
  ?v=<sha>&mobile=on&portraitlayout=1&perfprobe=1

Optional reset on load: &perfreset=1
In-game: tap top-left overlay box to reset rolling stats.

Shows: BUILD_ID, FPS, frame avg/p95/worst, >33ms/>50ms counts,
drawScene/minimap/chrome/hud ms, sprite count, district, seed,
canvas/css size, dpr, mob/port layout.

No overlay without perfprobe=1.

D2 fix: crSetSelectedStartDistrict accepts 2, '2', 'D2'.

Local proof: proof-perfprobe-solidwalls1-play.png (D1 seed 646848659).
